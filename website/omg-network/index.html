The OMG Network at a Glance

The OMG Network allows blockchain scalability using plasma architecture. Plasma raises transaction volume and speed without compromising on the security that a blockchain ledger provides.

Plasma
architecture

The OMG Networks More Viable Plasma (MoreVP) raises transaction volume and speed without compromising on the security that a blockchain ledger provides. Level up your business transactions from Ethereums double digits to over thousands on the OMG Network.

Trustless
scaling

More Viable Plasmaprovides trustless scaling. Your funds are secure even without a centralized governing authority. Take charge of your on-chain transactions with full authority over your keys and do business on a network built to last.

Permissionless
scaling

An open-source solution that lets you do business on your own terms. The OMG Networks permissionless environment empowers you to make the best transaction decisions for your business/project. Enter and exit your funds as you please

More Viable Plasma (MoreVP) lets you do ore on a blockchain. Build on an unstoppable financial infrastructure with trustless and permissionless scaling.

01

Maximum Throughput

Thanks to OMG Network More Viable Plasma, transactions are processed faster at larger volumes than when transacting on Ethereum.

02

Ethereum-level Security

Double security layer: We couple the speed of Proof of Authority (PoA) with Ethereums own security guarantees. An additional layer of security is offered through the decentralization of watchers, ensuring user funds remain safe on Plasma no matter what.

03

1/10th the Operational Cost

Users can expect to pay up to 90% lower fees than Ethereum for payments made on our network.

04

Seamless Integration

Focus on optimizing application instead of spending time integrating. With a seamless UI/UX design, tutorials, documentation and 3rd party integration support, integrations are made easy.

05

High Adaptability

Our technology is extensible for many use-cases (payment, remittances, video gaming economy, loyalty points), and interoperable with other infrastructures.

06

Regulatory Support

OMG Network Plasma architecture makes it easier for projects to be regulatory compliant. Validate and demonstrate correct exchange execution and make use of an on-chain audit trail that decreases the cost of regulatory obligations.
